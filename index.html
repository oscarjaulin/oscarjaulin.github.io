About Me

I hold a Ph.D. in Economics from Universidad Carlos III de Madrid. My research focuses on Applied Macroeconomics, International Economics, and Monetary Policy. I am currently a Junior Research Economist in the Macroeconomic Modelling Department at Banco de la República de Colombia. My previous professional experience includes work in macroeconomic forecasting and financial stability, with an emphasis on data-driven analysis and policy-oriented research.

On this website, you can find information about my ongoing research projects, my CV, and how to contact me.
